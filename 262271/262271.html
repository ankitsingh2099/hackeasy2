Digene Fizz

Digene Fizz is a powder that provides quick relief from acidity and gas. It comes in a convenient sachet that can be easily mixed with water. Digene Fizz is available in a variety of flavors, including orange, lemon, and mint.

Benefits

  • Quick relief from acidity and gas
  • Convenient sachet that can be easily mixed with water
  • Available in a variety of flavors

Directions

  1. Empty one sachet into a glass of water.
  2. Stir until dissolved.
  3. Drink immediately.

Warnings

  • Do not take more than the recommended dose.
  • If symptoms persist, consult your doctor.
  • Not recommended for children under 12 years of age.

Ingredients

  • Sodium bicarbonate
  • Citric acid
  • Magnesium carbonate
  • Sucralose
  • Acesulfame potassium
  • Flavor
