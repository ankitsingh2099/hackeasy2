Nozal Medicated Soap

Nozal Medicated Soap is an antifungal soap that is used to treat a variety of skin conditions, such as ringworm, athlete's foot, and jock itch. It contains the active ingredient ketoconazole, which is an antifungal agent that works by killing the fungus that causes these infections. Nozal Medicated Soap is available over-the-counter and can be used by adults and children 2 years of age and older.

Benefits of Nozal Medicated Soap

  • Kills the fungus that causes skin infections
  • Relieves itching and burning
  • Helps to clear up skin infections
  • Is safe for use by adults and children 2 years of age and older

How to Use Nozal Medicated Soap

  1. Wet the affected area with warm water.
  2. Apply a small amount of Nozal Medicated Soap to the affected area.
  3. Rub the soap into the skin for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Rinse the soap off with warm water.
  5. Repeat this process 1-2 times per day.

Precautions

  • Do not use Nozal Medicated Soap if you are allergic to any of its ingredients.
  • Do not use Nozal Medicated Soap on open wounds or cuts.
  • Do not use Nozal Medicated Soap for more than 4 weeks without consulting a doctor.
  • If you experience any side effects from using Nozal Medicated Soap, stop using it and talk to your doctor.
