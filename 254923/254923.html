Product Page

This is an OTC product. It contains 540mg of Magaldrate IP, 50mg of Simethicone IP and 10mg of Oxetacaine BP. It is used for the treatment of acidity, gas and stomach pain.

  • Magaldrate IP: 540mg
  • Simethicone IP: 50mg
  • Oxetacaine BP: 10mg

Dosage: Adults: One to two teaspoonfuls (5-10 ml.) 3 to 4 times daily, preferably after meals and at bedtime. Not recommended for children unless specifically directed by the physician.

Caution: Do not exceed the recommended dosage. Store protected from light at a temperature not exceeding 25oC. Do not freeze. Keep the medicine out of reach of children.

Warning: To be sold by retail on the prescription of a Registered Medical Practitioner only.
